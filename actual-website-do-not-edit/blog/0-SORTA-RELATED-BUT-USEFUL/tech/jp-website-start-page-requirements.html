What our team looks for in a start page

  • What benefit? (what makes this website different + proof (not just claims) of usefulness to the readers life)
    Why? We expect our readers to be intelligent; to value their time. Hence they; - Want to determine whether a page is useful / worth reading within a few seconds
  • Are there wasted words? Is it overly wordy or just straight facts/thoughts?
  • Are the topics being covered relevant to my life?
  • Is the content new and unique?
  • Is the website and all of its pages catered towards a great, easy, fast to consume experience for me, the reader?
    --> Is it logically organised? Nice headers, bullet points, clearly seperated sections and sentences / paragraphs
