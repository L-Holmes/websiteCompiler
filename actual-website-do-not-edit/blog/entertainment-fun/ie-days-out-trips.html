Days out & trips

Primary aim

Something that:
  • Provides great emotional resonance (most important!)
  • Does something for the person you are with. (e.g. If they've always wanted to go the the eisendrache castle...)
  • Has high probability of providing at least 1 or 2 exciting shared moments
    • e.g. hole in one; hitting bullseye; hitting strike; sunset; etc.
How this can be done / additional things that may be nice to incorporate
  • Unfamiliar / slightly novel setting
  • Away from stressors (like busy roads etc...)
  • Variety
Actually have fun!
  • This is a good time to give meaning to life / create good memories and stories
  • These subconsciously pass down, and can benefit social situations in the future- if you use them right and do the right things!

How people experience interesting things

  • Unique (looks different / never experienced before / slightly different etc.)
  • Variety (lots of different things)
  • Relative to everyday life (you are comparing subconsciously against what you are used to)
  • * This is a repeating point for a lot of human psychology! *
  • Does it matter how different it is?
  • i.e. Creating natural 'highs'
  • + Big emotional social aspect as well....

Point about emotional moments

Humans social creatures → Create social moments

For our team personally

Evaluated many of our trips.
What it isn't;
  • Money based
  • Driving / Big travels
  • Massive big changes
  • Lots of lesser moments, for a stereotypical 'Big High'
What it is;
  • The strongest days out are the ones that are a backdrop for social connection.
  • The best ones;
    • Create at least the minimum amount of scenery change and novelty to light up the brain
    • Give many opportunities for high-points. Times to celebrate with the people around you. And to talk about.
    • Give things to talk about

Planning

  • Time
  • Money
  • Most people only have 1 big holiday per year...
