Key Point: Maximise the amount of productive time vs. non-productive time Reminder: - 'Productive time' when de-stressing = the time needed to reach a baseline level of non-stressed, satisfied, contentness E.g. 1) - Lets approximate that it takes 20 mins of aerobic excercise Options: 1) Parkrun, 30 min drive away = *2 hours* of time used! - [Missed out on sleep, because wake up early] - 30 mins drive - 20 mins to park early - 40 mins to parkrun and back to car - 30 mins drive back - 30 mins get in, shower, etc. 2) Local run, from door = *50 mins* of time used! - 20 mins to run - 30 mins to get in, shower, etc. --------------------------------- = 1h 10m time saved! E.g. 2) - Lets approximate that it takes a 20 min to return to baseline 1) Interesting walk in the lake district = *4h 25m*! - 45 mins drive + parkup - 3 hours walk - 40 mins back 2) Local walk from house = 20 mins! - 20 mins walk --------------------------------- = 4h 5m time saved!
