The negativity score

aka happiness-score?

What is the 'negativity score'?

The negativity score is a twin to the idea of happiness.
It argues that...
And that is controversial! Many people hear that and think it is boring. I would never live my life like that.
  • Highs and lows keep me chasing, keep me working.
It is saying "Instead of a life with great highs and many lows, I'd rather have less lows, and live contently."
It is not depression, it is contentness.
  • Contentness is like the positive shine to depression's darkness.
  • Instead of nothing being interesting, everything is okay, nice and delightful.
[diagram of highs and lows.. vs. the more neutral negativity score]

It is a measure

  • It is a measure of how much negativity is impacting your life
  • It is a measure that some people use to rate how good their life is.

Where the concept comes from

It is a concept formed from thinking backwards;
  • Regrets, and how many old, wise people wish they would have lived their lives...
  • And what many people ultimately strive for after gaining that wisdom.

What a low negativity score looks like

With a low negativity score (good), you will never complain. Never feel down.
  • Small moments of joy in life will turn big.
  • You will ironically be able to gain great pleasure from the true lasting sources, such as helping others, and feeling like you are important to society.
  • Every day you will look back on the previous day and be grateful of what you did that day.
  • That to me is an ideal life.
There are very few intelligent people who stopped doing drugs, who is now in their 80s who encourages that life to others.
  • Because with great highs come great lows. Baseline dopamine.
  • But with low highs and low lows, the highs actually shine more prominently.
  • And you can then focus on building those highs, without the sacrifice of the lows.

How it is measured

Takes into account:
  • Peak lifetime negativity (bigger negativity is much worse than smaller, more persistent)
  • + the score over time...

One thing that often gets missed

The idea of things like loneliness and boredom
  • These are also negatives.
  • .. and stress whilst forcing yourself to work for a better future...
  • Its all part of it...

Separately, the 'ethical score'

  • The intelligence of something being harmed
  • Death is not bad. Suffering is bad.
  • Sometimes death can cause suffering to those around...

What AI won't tell you

  • AI doesn't tell you what questions you should ask
  • AI doesn't tell you your mindset
  • AI doesn't optimise for logic and rationality
  • AI doesn't arrange things in a way that are easy to go back to and check
  • AI doesn't mention new unique thoughts
