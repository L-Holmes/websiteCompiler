Accessibility Statement

We want this website to be accessible to as many people as possible. This includes people with visual, motor, auditory, or cognitive impairments.
Last updated: 25/03/2026

Our commitment

We aim to make this website as accessible as possible. We work towards conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA where possible.
We are a small operation and may not always achieve full compliance immediately, but we are committed to ongoing improvement.

Accessibility features
