Why Help Us Out / Why Donate

What donations go towards

Donations directly help us keep running and growing. This includes:
  • Keeping the website and blog free to access
  • Covering production costs for handmade products
  • Funding research and writing time for new blog content
  • Improving the site and expanding what we offer

Donations are entirely voluntary

There is no obligation to donate. You will receive the same access to content and the same service whether you donate or not.
By donating you are not:
  • Purchasing a product or service
  • Acquiring any ownership, equity, or financial interest in this business
  • Entitled to any financial return
  • Guaranteed any specific outcome, result, or benefit

Refunds on donations

Donations are generally non-refundable. If you believe a donation was made in error, please contact us as soon as possible and we will consider the situation on a case-by-case basis. We are not obligated to refund voluntary donations but will try to be reasonable where we can.

Transparency

We are a small UK-based sole trader operation. We are not a registered charity. Donations are received as income to the business and used at our discretion to support its operation and growth, as described above.
We do not publish detailed accounts of how each donation is spent. If this changes in the future we will update this page.

Future updates

This page will be updated if the business structure changes (e.g. incorporation as a limited company, or registration as a charity).
