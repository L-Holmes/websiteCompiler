- Go cold turkey. - As long as it is on your mind, resisting is difficult. - Its the habit that is hardest to break. Let the dopamine wear down. - It will feel impossible at first, and that your body is reacting stronger than others. But that is because you are in the loop.

General strategies

- Go somewhere where you are physically unable to access the thing. - E.g. a 2 week holiday (or ideally even longer if possible) - Be very wary when you get back! - If you can afford to have internet blockers... consider doing that... although this is generally an imperfect system. - If you can have an accountability partner, do it!

Harsh truths

- Self doubt / regret / sadness are not going to stop you. - If you're clever, you'll get around (internet) restrictions that you yourself have setup. - Time / Location (e.g. Not on your phone!) restrictions are better (e.g. 10 mins) - Whitelist even better. - Accountability partner even better...
