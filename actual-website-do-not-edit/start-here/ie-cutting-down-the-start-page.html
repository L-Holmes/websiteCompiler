They care about the results. - Show, don't tell. - Don't say we 'aim to minimise your time wasted' - Add that as a footnote in an extra info page, sure... - BUT MAINLY just show they that with conciseness! - Don't say we are a guru knowledge repository... - Show it by linking the guru stuff! Let them make that judgement! - Reader must assume that any statement without evidence is false. - That is just what a smart person does - In our untrustworthy world, especially on the web. - We just filter it out.
