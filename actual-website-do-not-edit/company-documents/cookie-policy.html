Cookie Policy

This policy explains how this website uses cookies. We comply with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).
Last updated: [DATE]

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They serve various functions including remembering preferences, maintaining sessions, and tracking behaviour for analytics.

Cookies we use

We do not currently use tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies on this website.
This website may use essential technical cookies only, such as:
  • Session cookies — to maintain basic website functionality during your visit
  • Preference cookies — to remember settings you have chosen *
Essential cookies do not require your consent under PECR as they are strictly necessary for the website to function.

Third-party cookies

Our payment processors (e.g. Stripe, Google Pay) may set their own cookies when you interact with a payment flow. These are governed by the respective provider's own cookie and privacy policies:
  • Stripe: stripe.com/gb/privacy
  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy

How to control cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
  • View cookies currently stored
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
Note: blocking essential cookies may affect website functionality. For browser-specific instructions, visit aboutcookies.org.

Future updates

This policy will be updated if we add analytics tools, embedded content, or any other service that introduces additional cookies.
