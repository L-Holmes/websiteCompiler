Refunds & chargebacks

Donations are voluntary and generally non-refundable. If you believe a donation was made in error, contact us as soon as possible. We will review on a case-by-case basis.
If you raise a chargeback without contacting us first, we reserve the right to contest it with evidence of the transaction. We take fraudulent chargebacks seriously.

Fraud prevention

Our payment processors use industry-standard fraud detection. Any suspected fraudulent activity will be reported to the relevant processor and, where appropriate, to the relevant authorities.
