# answer 1

https://ads.google.com
 * Tools > Keyword Planner > Discover new keywords
 * Input string: how, what, why, when, where, who, which, is, can, do, vs, difference
 * Click Get results
 * Sort: Avg. monthly searches

https://studio.youtube.com
 * Analytics > Inspiration
 * Search across YouTube
 * Input: how to, what is, why, vs, difference, is it
 * Filter: Content gaps

https://trends.google.com
 * Explore
 * Leave search term blank
 * Related Queries > Top

https://www.semrush.com
 * Keyword Magic Tool
 * Input: how
 * Toggle: Questions
 * Sort: Volume
 * Repeat for: what, why, is, can, do, vs, difference

https://ahrefs.com
 * Keywords Explorer
 * Input: how, what, why, who, where, when, can, is
 * Matching terms
 * Toggle: Questions
 * Sort: Volume

https://answersocrates.com
 * Input broad niche keyword
 * Search

https://meetglimpse.com/top-searched/most-searched-questions
 * Access report
 * Sort: Search Volume
