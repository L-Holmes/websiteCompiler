
# === Video idea generation ===
- Copy others
    - Identify certain youtubers... e.g.
        - In a given niche
        - Or.. Who just seem to be doing well in general
        - Or.. Who have been doing well recently
        - Or.. Just make videos which aren't that good... or could be done much better, without too much effort...
    - Find their outliers
        - Top viewed videos... 
            ->  + break down what made that vid good... apply that formula to not only copy, but also generate new similar video ideas...
- Identify good niches:
    - High click return niches
    - Niche's with vast target audience... / generally attract big views easily...
    - Niches like to attract customers that would then have high probability of buying products / merch from you...
    - Niches where the typical creators are poor / not much competition...
- Look at what's trending...
    - e.g. on news / youtube trends / google search etc.
- Go product first
    - Think what i can sell
    - then make videos targetting those people who would buy that thing...
