Complaints

If you have a complaint, please email us with:
  • Your name and order number (if applicable)
  • A clear description of the issue
  • What outcome you are looking for
  • Explicitly write "Complaint" in the subject line
We will try to resolve all complaints fairly and promptly. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may be able to escalate to an alternative dispute resolution (ADR) scheme or the relevant consumer authority.

Before contacting us

We receive a high volume of messages, many of which are non-essential or based on general curiosity or confusion. To help us prioritise effectively, please read the following before getting in touch.

Response times

We do not have a minimum or guaranteed response time.
We are a small operation and cannot guarantee a response to every message, but we do our best.
We cannot guarantee an extremely detailed response to every inquiry
In the future, we aim to introduce a system where emails are assigned a unique ID, allowing you to estimate when you might receive a response based on our current backlog.
We are not obligated to respond to every enquiry received.

Why you may not receive a response

Due to the volume of enquiries we receive, we are unable to respond to every message, or to provide detailed replies in all cases.
  • Many enquiries are non-essential or exploratory in nature
  • Some questions relate to information that may already be available or clarified elsewhere
  • Operational priorities take precedence over general enquiries
This approach allows us to focus on delivering and maintaining the core aspects of our service, rather than diverting resources away from them.
