Returns policy

Under the UK Consumer Contracts Regulations, you have the right to cancel most orders within 14 days of receiving your item, without giving a reason.
  • To exercise your right to cancel, you must inform us within 14 days of receiving your order
  • You then have a further 14 days to return the item to us
  • Items must be returned in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging where possible
  • You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item is faulty or we sent the wrong item
  • Custom or personalised items may not be eligible for return under the cancellation right — we will make this clear at point of purchase where applicable
If an item arrives damaged or faulty, please contact us within [X] days with a description and photo. We will arrange a replacement or refund.

Refund policy

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used
