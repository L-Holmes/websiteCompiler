Product Care & Safety

Our products are handmade in the UK using natural materials. Please follow the guidance below to get the most from them and ensure safe use.

Natural material variation

Because our products are handmade from natural materials, minor variations in colour, texture, grain, and finish are normal and expected. These are not defects — they are characteristics of the material.

Shoes

  • Keep away from prolonged moisture and direct heat (e.g. radiators, direct sunlight)
  • Allow shoes to dry naturally if wet — do not force dry
  • Use appropriate shoe trees to maintain shape when not in use
  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth. Use leather-appropriate cleaning products only where indicated
  • Condition leather periodically to prevent cracking and dryness
  • Do not machine wash
  • Wear on appropriate surfaces — our shoes are not designed for heavy industrial or outdoor terrain use

Small leather goods

  • Keep away from water and moisture where possible
  • Store away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth
  • Condition leather periodically with a suitable product
  • Do not overload wallets, card holders, or small bags beyond their intended capacity — this can distort the shape and damage stitching
  • Store flat or upright — do not stack heavy items on top of leather goods

Furniture / tables

  • Always check weight limits specified in the individual product listing before use *
  • Do not exceed specified weight limits
  • Keep away from prolonged moisture and direct heat sources
  • Wipe clean with a slightly damp cloth — avoid harsh chemical cleaners unless specified
  • Use felt pads or coasters to protect surfaces
  • Do not attempt to modify or structurally alter the furniture — this voids any liability on our part for subsequent damage or injury
  • Ensure furniture is placed on a stable, level surface
  • Inspect periodically for signs of wear, loose joints, or structural issues

General safety

  • All products must be used for their intended purpose only
  • Keep packaging materials away from young children
  • If a product is damaged on arrival or develops a fault, stop using it and contact us
  • Do not attempt to repair products yourself in ways that could compromise structural integrity or safety
